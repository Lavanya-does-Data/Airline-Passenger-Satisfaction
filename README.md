# Airline-Passenger-Satisfaction
This airline business Power BI project depicting line graph showing count of gender & sum of age by type of travel along with two slicers for age & type of travel; column graphs showing count of gender by type of travel along with slicers for age, type of travel & two cards for on-board service & count of online boarding; a pie graph showing count of satisfaction by gender & another pie graph showing count of satisfaction by type of travel along with two slicers showing age & type of travel.
Data source: https://mavenanalytics.io/data-playground/airline-passenger-satisfaction
